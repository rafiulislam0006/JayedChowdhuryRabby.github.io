Recruitment partners

ApplyBoard simplifies the discovery, application, and acceptance process for your student.

Step 1

Create Student Profile and get Matched

The ApplyBoard Platform matches students to programs and institutions based on their unique background and interests. Complete a single universal application and apply your students to multiple programs, schools, and countries at once.

Step 2

We Review and Submit Student Applications

The ApplyBoard Team checks each application received to ensure they are complete. Once reviewed, applications are submitted by ApplyBoard to our Partner Schools for review.

Step 3

Your Student Gets a Letter of Acceptance

By using ApplyBoard, your students can expect a 95% chance of acceptance to their institution of choice! Once ApplyBoard receives the LOA, we will notify you and forward you their admission package
